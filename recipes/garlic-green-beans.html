Buttery Garlic Green Beans

Description

This is a side dish that could go well with many different kinds of meals.

Ingredients

  • green beans
  • butter
  • garlic
  • lemon pepper
  • salt

Steps

  1. Place beans in skillet with water and cover
  2. Bring to boil, then reduce and simmer until beans soften (5 minutes).
  3. Drain water
  4. Add butter and cook until butter is melted
  5. Stir in garlic and cook until fragrant
  6. Season with lemon pepper and salt
