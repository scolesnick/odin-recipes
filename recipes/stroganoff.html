Beef Stroganoff

Description

Beef Stroganoff is a creamy, savory, pasta dish.

Ingredients

  • steak strips
  • large onion
  • butter
  • mushrooms
  • flour
  • beef broth
  • dijon mustard
  • sour cream
  • oil
  • salt/pepper

Steps

  1. Brown the steak strips and transfer to plate/bowl
  2. Cook onion with butter for 1 minute, then add mushrooms
  3. Cook mushrooms until golden, add flour and stir
  4. Add broth
  5. Stir in sour cream and mustard
  6. Bring to simmer, then reduce. Add s&p to taste once thickened.
  7. Add beef back in
