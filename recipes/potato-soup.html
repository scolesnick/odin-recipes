Baked Potato Soup

Description

This is a savory, creamy, soup.

Ingredients

  • bacon
  • butter
  • milk
  • large baked potatoes peeled and cubed
  • flour
  • chopped green onions
  • shredded cheddar cheese
  • sour cream
  • salt/pepper

  1. Brown bacon and crumble.
  2. In stock pot, melt butter, whisk in flour.
  3. Stir in milk, whisk until thickened
  4. Stir in potatoes and onions
  5. Bring to boil and reduce, simmer for 10 minutes
  6. Add bacon, cheese, sour cream, salt and pepper
  7. Serve once cheese is melted
